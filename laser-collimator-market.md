<p><p>The laser collimator market comprises various types, including FC/PC, FC/APC, and SMA905 connectors. FC/PC (Ferrule Connector/Physical Contact) offers low insertion loss and is used in high-density applications. FC/APC (Angled Physical Contact) features an angled end face that minimizes back reflections, ideal for laser applications requiring high precision. SMA905 connectors are widely used in fiber optics, offering versatility and ease of use, suitable for various applications. Each type serves specific needs in optical systems and connectivity.</p></p>
