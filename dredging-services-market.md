<p><strong>The Dredging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Dredging</li><li>Mechanical Dredging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dredging services encompass two primary market types: hydraulic dredging and mechanical dredging. Hydraulic dredging utilizes water-powered suction to remove sediment, making it effective for deeper and softer materials. It’s primarily used in channels and ports. In contrast, mechanical dredging employs equipment like clamshells or draglines to physically scoop or excavate materials, suited for tougher, denser substances in shallower waters. Both methods play essential roles in maintaining waterways, construction projects, and environmental restoration.</p></p>
