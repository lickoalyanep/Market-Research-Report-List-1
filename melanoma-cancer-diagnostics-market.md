<p><strong>The Melanoma Cancer Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Tests</li><li>Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) Tests</li><li>Immunohistochemical (IHC) Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melanoma cancer diagnostics market includes various advanced testing modalities. Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) tests identify specific genetic alterations by using fluorescent probes, allowing for precise tumor profiling. Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) tests analyze DNA copy number variations, providing insights into chromosomal abnormalities associated with melanoma. Immunohistochemical (IHC) tests utilize antibodies to detect specific proteins in tumor samples, aiding in classification and prognosis. Together, these tests enhance diagnostic accuracy and inform treatment strategies for melanoma patients.</p></p>
