<p><p>The RF Duplexer market includes three main types based on semiconductor materials: Silicon, Gallium Arsenide (GaAs), and Silicon-Germanium (SiGe). Silicon duplexers are cost-effective and widely used in various applications due to their good performance. GaAs duplexers offer superior efficiency and higher frequency capabilities, making them ideal for advanced communication systems. SiGe duplexers combine benefits of both, providing enhanced performance at microwave frequencies while maintaining lower costs than GaAs, thus catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
