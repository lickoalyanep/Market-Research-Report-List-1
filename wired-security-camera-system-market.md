<p><ul><li>DVR (Digital Video Recorder) Systems</li><li>NVR (Network Video Recorder) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wired Security Camera System market features two primary types: DVR (Digital Video Recorder) and NVR (Network Video Recorder) systems. DVR systems capture and store video footage from analog cameras via coaxial cables, focusing on ease of use and affordability. In contrast, NVR systems work with IP cameras, utilizing Ethernet connections for higher-quality video and advanced features like remote access and smart analytics. Each system caters to different needs, with DVR being more traditional and NVR offering greater flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
