<p><strong>The Silicon Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Type</li><li>Citrate-soluble Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon fertilizer market is primarily categorized into two types: water-soluble and citrate-soluble. Water-soluble silicon fertilizers dissolve easily in water, making them suitable for foliar application and enhancing nutrient uptake in crops. Citrate-soluble silicon fertilizers, on the other hand, are designed to release silicon when in contact with citric acid, improving soil health and plant resistance to stress. Both types play crucial roles in promoting plant growth, increasing yield, and enhancing overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
