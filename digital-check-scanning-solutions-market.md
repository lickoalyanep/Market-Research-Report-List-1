<p><p>The digital check scanning solutions market is divided into two main types: single-feed and multi-feed check scanning systems. Single-feed check scanning systems process one check at a time, making them ideal for low-volume operations where ease of use and compact size are priorities. In contrast, multi-feed check scanning systems can handle multiple checks simultaneously, catering to high-volume environments like banks and retail operations. This flexibility enhances efficiency by reducing processing time and streamlining workflow in financial transactions.</p></p>
