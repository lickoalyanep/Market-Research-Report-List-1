<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA)</li><li>Enzyme linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor-standing automated immunoassay analyzer market encompasses several assay types. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) utilizes light emission for detection, offering high sensitivity and quick results. Enzyme-Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) combines enzyme reactions with fluorescence for quantitative analysis. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a widely used method for detecting and quantifying proteins. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) employs radioactively labeled substances, though its use has declined due to safety concerns. Other assays may include multiplex assays or novel techniques, catering to diverse diagnostic needs.</p></p>
