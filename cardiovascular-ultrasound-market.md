<p><p>The cardiovascular ultrasound market includes various types such as Transthoracic Echocardiogram (TTE), Transesophageal Echocardiogram (TEE), and Stress Echocardiogram. TTE is a non-invasive test that uses sound waves to create images of the heart from the chest wall. TEE involves inserting a probe down the esophagus for clearer heart images. Stress Echocardiograms assess heart function under physical stress. The 'Others' category captures emerging techniques and specialized applications in cardiovascular imaging, expanding diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
