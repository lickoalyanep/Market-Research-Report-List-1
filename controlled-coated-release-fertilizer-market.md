<p><ul><li>Polymer-Coated Products</li><li>Non Polymer-Coated Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Controlled (coated) release fertilizers are designed to release nutrients gradually, optimizing plant uptake and minimizing environmental impact. The market is segmented into polymer-coated products and non-polymer-coated products. Polymer-coated fertilizers utilize a polymer layer to control nutrient release rates, offering tailored feeding schedules for various crops. Non-polymer-coated products, on the other hand, use materials like sulfur or calcium to slow nutrient release, often at a lower cost. Both types enhance efficiency but differ in application, longevity, and nutrient delivery methods.</p></p>
